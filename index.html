About

More About Me

I am currently a Doctoral Researcher at the Vrije Universiteit Brussels.
My Ph.D. advisor is Prof. dr. Tias Guns.

I hold a Master's degree in Data Analytics from Indian Statistical Institute
and a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur.


My research focus is on machine learning, deep learning and constraint programming. My current research interests include development of learning algorithms that combines prediction and optimization in a single framework.
